SAGE: STEM-MUN Articulate Grand Essays 2024

We are thrilled to announce the "SAGE: STEM-MUN Articulate Grand Essays 2024," the first Egyptian articles competition organized by high schoolers to open its doors to participants from around the world. This mind-blowing event invites young minds globally to engage in thoughtful writing and contribute their perspectives on some of the most critical issues facing our society today. Don't hesitate to join our fascinating event, and to put your voice to be a part of the change
