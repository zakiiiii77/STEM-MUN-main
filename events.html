Our Programs

Explore our specialized educational tracks designed to enhance your MUN experience

Political Science Program

The Political Science Program introduces students to the fundamental principles of political theory, diplomacy, governance systems, and international affairs. Through interactive workshops and simulations, participants explore how political decisions are made on both national and global scales, preparing them for effective debate, negotiation, and leadership in MUN conferences.

SDGs Program

The SDGs (Sustainable Development Goals) Program educates students on the 17 global goals set by the United Nations. Participants dive into challenges such as poverty, climate change, education, and gender equality, learning how to incorporate SDG targets into MUN discussions and resolutions. It empowers delegates to advocate for sustainable development through diplomatic dialogue.

Research Track

The Research Track in STEM MUN is designed to enhance delegates' understanding of global issues by guiding them through in-depth research processes. Participants learn how to write policy papers, understand international relations frameworks, analyze real-world conflicts, and formulate well-informed positions based on their assigned countries' perspectives. It builds the foundation for confident and accurate MUN performance.
